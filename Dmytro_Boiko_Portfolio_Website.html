Audience

My digital portfolio is designed primarily for university admissions tutors and hiring managers in tech and creative media. Other viewers include tutors, freelance clients, friends, and potential mentors. They expect to see technical skills, completed projects, professional layout, personality, and downloadable resources.

Purpose

The portfolio showcases my best work and readiness for education or employment. It acts as a visual CV and professional platform. It helps viewers by saving time, proving skills, building trust, and supporting decisions.

Justification of Final Design

The chosen design is clean, responsive, and professional. It ensures accessibility, structured content, visual consistency, and clear communication. It meets the needs of both academic and professional audiences.
